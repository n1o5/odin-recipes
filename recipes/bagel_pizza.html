Bagel Pizza


Description

These pizza bagels are a homemade take on a frozen favorite with much less fat. They're yummy!


    Ingredients
  • bagels
  • pizza toppings

      • Steps
  • Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  • Arrange bagels, cut-side up, on the prepared baking sheet. Spoon a thin layer of pizza sauce over each bagel half and sprinkle with pizza toppings.
  • Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and pepperoni is lightly browned, about 6 minutes.
